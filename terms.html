2. Description of Services

3. What Rial Does and Does Not Verify

What We Verify:

  • Device authenticity through hardware attestation
  • Capture timestamp accuracy
  • GPS coordinates (when enabled)
  • Image transformation history (crops, resizes, filters applied)
  • Cryptographic chain of custody

What We Do Not Verify:

  • Content truthfulness or accuracy
  • Whether a scene was staged or manipulated before capture
  • Identity of the photographer
  • Validity of insurance claims or legal assertions
  • Whether an image is AI-generated (we verify device origin, not content detection)
