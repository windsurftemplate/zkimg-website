Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") governs your use of Rial's services. By using our Services, you agree to comply with this Policy. Violations may result in suspension or termination of your access.

2. Permitted Uses

You may use Rial's Services for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Verifying authenticity of photos for insurance claims
  • Establishing chain of custody for legal proceedings
  • Documenting field work, inspections, or deliveries
  • Protecting against fraud in business operations
  • Verifying media authenticity for journalism
  • Building applications that require image verification
  • Research and educational purposes

3. Prohibited Uses

3.1 Illegal Activities

You may NOT use our Services to:

  • Violate any local, state, national, or international law
  • Engage in or facilitate illegal activities
  • Infringe on intellectual property rights of others
  • Violate privacy rights or data protection laws
  • Engage in fraud, misrepresentation, or deception

3.2 Harmful Content

You may NOT use our Services to verify, distribute, or facilitate:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child exploitation
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery ("revenge porn")
  • Content promoting violence, terrorism, or extremism
  • Harassment, stalking, or doxxing
  • Hate speech or discriminatory content

3.3 Abuse of Services

You may NOT:

  • Attempt to circumvent security measures or rate limits
  • Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble our software
  • Use automated means to access Services in violation of our Terms
  • Share API keys or credentials with unauthorized parties
  • Create multiple accounts to evade restrictions
  • Use Services to spam, phish, or distribute malware

3.4 Misrepresentation

You may NOT:

  • Misrepresent Rial's capabilities or verification results
  • Falsely claim photos are verified when they are not
  • Use our branding or logos without permission
  • Impersonate Rial or our employees
  • Make false or misleading claims about verification status

3.5 Device Tampering

You may NOT use Services on devices that have been:

  • Jailbroken or rooted
  • Modified to bypass hardware security features
  • Compromised by malware or unauthorized software
  • Configured to spoof GPS, time, or other metadata

Note: Verification on tampered devices may produce unreliable results and is a violation of this Policy.

4. Professional Responsibilities

4.1 Insurance and Claims Professionals

If using Rial for insurance claims verification:

  • Do not rely solely on Rial's verification for claim decisions
  • Conduct additional due diligence as required by your policies
  • Understand that Rial verifies device origin, not claim validity
  • Comply with all applicable insurance regulations

4.2 Legal Professionals

If using Rial for legal evidence:

  • Consult with legal counsel regarding admissibility in your jurisdiction
  • Maintain proper chain of custody documentation
  • Do not represent Rial's verification as legal advice
  • Understand the technical limitations of cryptographic verification

4.3 Healthcare Professionals

If using Rial in healthcare contexts:

  • Ensure HIPAA compliance for all patient-related imagery
  • Do not use Rial for medical diagnosis or treatment decisions
  • Verify regulatory compliance in your jurisdiction
  • Maintain appropriate security measures for patient data

4.4 Journalists and Media

If using Rial for media verification:

  • Conduct independent fact-checking beyond technical verification
  • Protect source anonymity as appropriate
  • Do not misrepresent verification results to the public

5. Rate Limits and Fair Use

5.1 API Usage

  • Respect rate limits specified in your plan
  • Do not attempt to circumvent rate limits through multiple accounts
  • Contact us if you need higher limits for legitimate use cases

5.2 Resource Consumption

  • Do not abuse computing resources (e.g., excessive proof generation)
  • Implement reasonable caching to minimize redundant verification requests
  • Report bugs or performance issues instead of exploiting them

6. Data Usage Restrictions

6.1 What You Can Do

  • Store cryptographic proofs for your own verification needs
  • Share verification results as needed for legitimate business purposes
  • Build applications that integrate Rial's verification

6.2 What You Cannot Do

  • Resell Rial's verification services without authorization
  • Create competing verification products using our technology
  • Extract or aggregate verification data for commercial resale
  • Use verification data to train machine learning models without permission

7. Reporting Violations

7.1 How to Report

If you become aware of violations of this Policy, report them to:

  • Email: abuse@rial.app
  • Include: Description of violation, relevant URLs or account information, evidence if available

7.2 What We'll Do

Upon receiving a report, we will:

  • Investigate the alleged violation
  • Take appropriate action (warning, suspension, or termination)
  • Notify you of the outcome if you were the reporter
  • Cooperate with law enforcement if illegal activity is involved

8. Enforcement

8.1 Violations

Violations of this Policy may result in:

  • Warning: First-time or minor violations
  • Temporary Suspension: Repeated or moderate violations
  • Permanent Termination: Serious or repeated violations
  • Legal Action: Illegal activities or significant harm

8.2 Appeals

If your account is suspended or terminated:

  • You may appeal by contacting appeals@rial.app
  • Include your account details and explanation
  • We will review appeals within 7 business days
  • Our decision is final

9. Law Enforcement Cooperation

We cooperate with law enforcement when required by law or when we believe in good faith that cooperation is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights or property
  • Prevent imminent harm to persons or property
  • Investigate potential criminal activity

10. Changes to This Policy

We may modify this Acceptable Use Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Continued use of Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified Policy.
